VyapariMithra

  • Vyapari Mithra helps manage chit, GDS, welfare schemes, memberships, and daily accounts in one platform with better accuracy.
  • Real-time recording, SMS alerts, transparency, fewer disputes, and increased trust for members and agents.
  • Simplifies operations with dedicated, easy-to-use interfaces for units, collection agents, and members, making it accessible even for non-technical users.
  • Reports for daily collections, profit & loss, and defaults are generated instantly. This helps management make faster and better financial decisions.
  • The system uses SSL security, certified APIs, and secure login access. Advanced protections prevent unauthorized access and data manipulation.
