Bulk SMS Service – FAQ

Bulk SMS Service – FAQ

What is Bulk SMS?

Bulk SMS is a service that allows businesses to send text messages to a large group of recipients simultaneously. It is widely used for marketing campaigns, notifications, and alerts.

How can Bulk SMS help my business?

Reach customers instantly, Promote offers and campaigns, Send important alerts and updates, Improve customer engagement

What types of SMS can I send?

We support both Promotional SMS (for marketing) and Transactional SMS (for OTPs, alerts, etc.) with high delivery rates.

What is the difference between Promotional and Transactional SMS?

Promotional SMS contains marketing content and is sent within DND hours. Transactional SMS is used for OTPs and alerts and can be delivered 24/7, even to DND numbers.

Can I integrate the SMS gateway into my own software?

Yes, we provide easy-to-use API documentation to connect our SMS gateway seamlessly with your CRM, website, or mobile application.

What is DLT Registration? Do you provide DLT registration assistance?

DLT (Distributed Ledger Technology) is a regulatory requirement in India for sending bulk SMS to ensure trusted buisness.Our team provides complete DLT setup and assistance, making the process simple and hassle-free for your business.Once your DLT registration and account setup are completed, you can start sending SMS within a short time.
