Website Development – FAQ

Website Development – FAQ

Why does my business need a website?

A website gives your business a professional online presence, helps customers find you on search engines, and builds credibility and trust. It also allows your business to generate leads and sales 24/7.

How long does it take to build a website?

Most websites are completed within 5–10 working days, depending on the project requirements, number of pages, and features needed.

Do you provide domain and hosting?

Yes. We provide complete website packages including domain registration and secure hosting, so you don’t have to worry about technical setup.

Can I update my website content later?

Yes. We provide easy-to-manage websites where you can update text, images, and products. We also provide support if you need assistance.

Can you integrate WhatsApp chat on the website?

Yes. We can integrate WhatsApp chat, allowing customers to contact your business instantly from the website, improving lead conversions.

Do you help businesses without any technical knowledge?

Yes. Our team handles everything from design to launch, making the process simple and hassle-free for business owners.
